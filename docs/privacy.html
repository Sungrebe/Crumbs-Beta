Privacy Policy

The TrailCrumbs App does not collect or store any personally identifiable or other sensitive information from its users. The App physically can't as there is no server database or back end to process and/or store it. What happens on your device stays on your device.

TrailCrumbs does not use any analytics software or third-party scripts either. The App doesn’t know who you are, where you’re coming from and does not want to.

What we collect, store

No storage or collection of user data. There is zero tracking on the App.
